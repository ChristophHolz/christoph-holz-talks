Digital Leadership — what we can learn from Winnetou

Thought Experiments on the New Leadership in the Age of AI

Did I become a boss to give orders — or to lead people? Is it too much to ask of AI that it take hierarchy off our hands and give leadership back what truly defines it? The Apaches knew this all along.

Three Thoughts from the Talk

What remains when AI takes over everything that can be optimized? The human being. With everything that cannot be optimized.

Thought experiments are the training camp for a successful future.

AI is not a technical term, but a philosophical creation myth. It represents computer science's eternal longing to create a life form of its own.

What Your Audience Takes Away from This Talk

Through vivid metaphors and examples, your guests grasp what artificial intelligence can do — and where its limits lie.
